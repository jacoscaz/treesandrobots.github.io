A common thread across all of my software projects is my constant effort
to make software _sustainable_: lowering overheads and friction, minimizing
dependencies and complexity, being considerate of CPU and memory, keeping
code predictable. 

## Current Projects

These are the projects I'm focusing on at the moment, mostly as their primary
maintainer and creator:

- [`grovekit`](https://github.com/grovekit):
  IoT platform and libraries for the [Homie MQTT convention].
- [`fondamenta`](https://github.com/jacoscaz/fondamenta):
  experimental agentic harness focused on epistemological continuity.
- [`runtyped`](https://github.com/runtyped/runtyped):
  framework for making TypeScript types fully available at runtime through
  reflection.
- [`typed-ocpp`](https://github.com/jacoscaz/typed-ocpp):
  parsing and validation of OCPP (1.6, 2.0.1 and 2.1) messages.
- [`@bacnet-js/device`](https://github.com/bacnet-js/device):
  implementing BACnet/IP devices in Node.js.

[Homie MQTT convention]: https://homieiot.github.io
