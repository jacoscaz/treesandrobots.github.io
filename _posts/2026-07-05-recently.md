This is the first issue of a series of posts called _Recently_, the concept
for which I first encountered while reading [Tom MacWright]'s excellent blog.

Each issue will essentially be a collection of links and short content typical
of [microblogging] posts, just published in batches every few weeks or so.
Microblogging that does not destroy our collective attention.

This first instance contains content I had accumulated so far in the separate
microblogging feed of this blog, which used to be called _syslog_.

[Tom MacWright]: https://macwright.org/
[microblogging]: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microblogging

## Books

[Sea of Tranquility] by [Emily St. John Mandel] is a glorious celebration
of longing and belonging, of beauty, of empathy. A graceful, intimate work of 
speculative fiction masterfully woven across time, confronting some of mankind's
fundamental questions with a sense of wonder that never dissipates. I'll have to 
read it again soon.

[Sea of Tranquility]: https://openlibrary.org/works/OL24731706W/Sea_of_Tranquility
[Emily St. John Mandel]: https://www.emilymandel.com

## Music

Spirits by [Nothing More]. An Evocative mix of post-screamo, hardcore and nu metal with accents of
djent and EDM. This is what happens when we embrace our teenage angst as
we grow up, learning how to wield it and focus it against the struggles
of adult life. **Loved** it. Highlights: "Ships in the Night", 
"Don't Look Back",  "Deja Vu",  "Dream With Me".

[Nothing More]: https://nothingmore.net
